# User Feature Guide: Gossiptoon Story & Webtoon Creator

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of what you can do with the Gossiptoon application. It allows you to transform simple story ideas into fully realized webtoon scripts and visual storyboards using advanced AI assistants.

## 1. Story Creation

The journey begins with your story idea. The application acts as your co-author to expand and refine your narrative.

### **Start with an Idea (The Seed)**

You have two main ways to begin:

- **Provide a Seed:** Input a specific prompt, a Reddit post ID, or a raw concept.
- **Create from Scratch:** Let the AI help you brainstorm a new story from nothing.

### **Select a Narrative Genre**

Choose a "Narrative Plugin" that frames how your story is told. This affects the dialogue style, setting, and emotional beats. Supported genres include:

- **Modern Romance:** _Modern Romance Drama, Workplace Romance, Campus/Youth Romance._
- **Fantasy & Historical:** _Fantasy Romance, Historical Period (Joseon), Reincarnation/Isekai._
- **Emotional Arcs:** _Childhood Friends to Lovers, Missed Connection, Second Chance._
- **Dramatic Scenarios:** _Confession Moment, Breakup/Makeup, Love Triangle Choice, Secret Relationship Reveal, Revenge Confrontation, Dark Obsessive Romance._

### **AI Story Writer**

Once you have a seed and genre:

- **Drafting:** The `Story Writer` AI generates a structured story (optimized for a 50-second short format).
- **Auto-Correction:** The system automatically reviews the story for emotional impact and structure, rewriting it if necessary to ensure high quality before you even see it.

---

## 2. Character Studio

Manage the cast of your stories with a dedicated Character Library.

### **Create & Customize**

- Define characters with specific details: **Name, Age, Gender, and Key Visual Features** (e.g., "long black hair," "scar on cheek").
- The system ensures these visual details remain consistent across every panel of your webtoon.

### **Character Library**

- **Save Characters:** Save your favorite characters to the "Library" for future use.
- **Load Characters:** valid Import existing characters into new stories so you don't have to recreate them from scratch.
- **Reference Images:** The system generates and stores a reference "Character Image" to guide the visual consistency of the story.

---

## 3. Webtoon Director (Visual Adaptation)

Transform your written story into a visual script and storyboard.

### **Virtual Director**

The application acts as a "Webtoon Director" to convert your text story into a panel-by-panel script.

- **Visual Hierarchy:** It automatically decides which moments need a **Wide Shot** (for context), a **Medium Shot** (for dialogue), or a **Close-Up** (for emotional impact).
- **Panel Layouts:** It generates dynamic layouts, choosing between:
  - **Single Panels:** For major impact moments.
  - **Vertical Sequences:** For emotional build-up or zooming in.
  - **Horizontal Splits:** For simultaneous reactions.
  - **Dynamic Grids:** For action sequences.

### **Select a Visual Style (The Lens)**

You can choose the artistic style for your webtoon images, separate from the story genre. Options include:

- **Art Styles:** _Soft Romantic Webtoon, Vibrant Fantasy, Dramatic Historical, Noir Romance, Painterly/Artistic._
- **Lighting & Atmosphere:** The style controls the color palette (pastels, dark tones), lighting (candlelight, neon, sunlight), and rendering (cel-shaded, semi-realistic).

### **Production Features**

- **Hero Shot (Thumbnail):** The system automatically identifies the most visually stunning or emotionally powerful moment in your story and marks it as the "Hero Shot." This generates an extra-detailed image suitable for a video thumbnail or cover art.
- **Scene Generation:** The system generates images for every scene/panel defined in the script.
- **Multi-Panel Generation:** Capable of creating complex multi-panel pages that look like real webtoon strips.
- **Consistency:** It uses your character definitions to keep faces and outfits consistent throughout the visuals.
- **Smart Audio/SFX:** The script distinguishes between spoken dialogue (for text-to-speech) and visual sound effects (SFX like _CRASH_, _BOOM_) to ensure the final output is audio-visually coherent.

---

## 4. Shorts Generation

In addition to full stories, you can generate **Shorts Scripts** specifically designed for 6-scene varying formats, perfect for quick social media content.

## 5. Summary of Workflow

1.  **Input:** You provide a story idea/seed.
2.  **Configuration:** You pick a **Story Genre** (Narrative) and a **Visual Style** (Art).
3.  **Writing:** The AI writes and polishes the story.
4.  **Casting:** You review/load characters from your library.
5.  **Directing:** The AI converts the story into a visual script with panels and shots.
6.  **Production:** The system generates the final character images, scene panels, and the Hero Shot.

**Result:** A complete story package with a script, character designs, and visual panels ready for viewing or further video production.
